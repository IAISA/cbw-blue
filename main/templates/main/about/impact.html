About Us

In Earn a Bike, students are paired with mentors for 24 hours of lessons in bike mechanics and safety.

While working on their bikes, students learn teamwork, perseverance and other life skills. They see the satisfaction of turning a broken bike into a functional one, and earn their refurbished bike and new helmet!Â 

Our Impact

Bike mentoring makes students feel more engaged at school, which can have an impact on academic success.

Of students at a United Way community school who completed Earn a Bike or JrEAB, 72% improved or maintained their school attendance that year!
